🎓 Course Outcomes

CO1

Analyze cooperative and non-cooperative game models in real-world strategic scenarios using simulation tools.

CO2

Simulate extensive form games with perfect information to develop strategy trees in agent-based environments.

CO3

Develop Bayesian or sequential strategies for imperfect information games using game-theoretic modeling platforms.

CO4

Implement a mechanism design system for social preference aggregation with transparency in computational scenarios.
